    <center><ul id="stepForVegetableSalad">
        <li>For an appetizer, plan on serving about 2–3 oz (55–85g) of (total) greens per person.</li>
        <li>Avoid the more common curly kale, which is tough to eat raw.</li>
        <li>Kale also benefits from "massaging" to break down the fibers. Place it in a bowl of acidic dressing and squeeze handfuls of leaves with moderate pressure</li>
        <li>Let sit for 5–15 minutes before serving.</li>
        <li>Add softer greens. A variety of textures is key to a good salad</li>
        <li>Add a roughly equal amount of arugula, spinach, butter leaf lettuce, or another soft, leafy green to your salad.</li>
        <li>Handle these gently while washing and chopping, as soft leaves tend to bruise more easily.</li>
